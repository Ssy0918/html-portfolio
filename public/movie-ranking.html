The Best Movies According to Shuyan Sun

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Coco

This film taught me the lesson that people only died when they are forgotten, and reminded me the importance of family.

Robot Dreams

It is a film without actor's lines, but when the music of "September" goes on, it makes you feel touched.

Delicious in Dungeon

I love the DnD themed animes, this one is really funny, and showed me a new perspective to view adventures.
